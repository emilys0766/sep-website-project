How to Draw a Face

Step 1- Draw a light circle/oval

So you wanna get into drawn, eh? Let’s start with the most expressive thing by far, the face. When you start drawing, always and I mean ALWAYS, draw lightly. This will make the lines easier to erase later on, where drawing a face is probably one of the easier things to draw out of the entire guide. Start small with a circle or an oval that is lightly drawn onto whatever you are drawing the face onto.




Step 2 - Lightly drawn cross and parallel lines



Now that you’ve drawn a lightly drawn shape, you add a cross and two parallel lines used to model how large the eyes will be and where they’ll go. This is extremely important if you want everything to look accurate to actual humans.










Step 3 - Details



You’ve done it, you reached the near end of the face tutorial! Now you gotta add those beautiful details to your drawing. With faces, you can add all kinds of shapes to the chin, as well as put any expression you want on these faces. The face is now your canvas!
