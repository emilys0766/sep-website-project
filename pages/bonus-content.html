Ecka - Rural X Mystic Future

This is a side project worked on by Deadbeat (Bali DeFreitas) and was heavily inspired by BoTW. Ecka takes place thousands of years after a meteorite crashed into Earth, it would’ve wiped all of society, however 100 people were chosen for Cryogenic Sleep and each choosing a different amount of years to sleep for. Our main character Yuka Igata had chosen to sleep for 3000 years and when he awakens the world he once knew is more wild like and covered in plants: Rural if one would. This starts the journey of our young valiant hero, in this game a player would be able to use almost anything as a weapon. Taking tree branches and other things to make other types of weapons to fend themselves against the corrupted humans of the now Rural world.

In this world, people have developed a cleaner and more powerful version of energy within everything. This is known as Chi and it comes in both Positive and Negative sets, with Positive being more oriented to healing things or yourself and Negative Chi would be more closer to the usage of damaging foes and different form of combat.

Ecka is known for all kinds of weapons that can both be made or found throughout the world. From Clubs to Guns, it would also have a crafting system for armors and weapons. As time progresses you get to witness the creation of a near indestructible force or you can always become a stealth master and hide within the shadows.

In short Ecka is planned to be a very gameplay driven style kind of game with the story taking a much needed backseat similar to it’s inspiration.
