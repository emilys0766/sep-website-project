How to Draw Hands and Feet!

Step 1 - Draw circles or rectangles

When you are drawing feet or shoes, always draw rectangles as the base of your foot. You could always draw circles as well, this helps with creating the natural shape of the foot or shoe. This also applies to hands in a sense of drawing a square or a circle for the base.
