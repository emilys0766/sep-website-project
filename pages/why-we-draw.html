Why We Draw!

The two of us draw for different reasons, yet somehow they can easily intertwine into each other.

Bali (AKA: Deadbeat): My personal reason for drawing is the factor that I wanna become a great animator like those in the animating business and as well as I enjoy drawing more than anything else. Drawing was a hobby of mine since I was five years old and I wanna keep trying to improve my skills as time progresses.

Emily (AKA: Aqua): At first, I started learning how to draw when I became obbessed with video games and anime (Ex: Sonic the Hedgehog, Dragon Ball Z, etc). I wanted to become a game designer or animator and create a media similar to the kind of content I consumed as a kid. As I grew older, I started to take my hobby of drawing a lot more seriously and even created my own original characters (seen in the gallery below) and story. I hope to be able to create my own website and web comic one day with my art!
